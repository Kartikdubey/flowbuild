Introducing FlowSim ...

FlowSim is Flowgrammable's visual OpenFlow simulator. Explore the details of OpenFlow by configuring a switch dataplane and obversing packets transition through the pipeline. Some of the many capabilities include ...

  • Custom switch profile builder, emulate a specific vendor
  • Custom packet builder
  • Packet trace designer
  • OpenFlow dataplane configuration
  • OpenFlow dataplane visualization
  • Single step packets through the dataplane pipeline
